# Talaria design archetypes

Eight visual systems keyed to the network's verticals. Classify a site's topic, take the
archetype's pairing + personality + signature idea, then lock the accent by hashing the domain
into the archetype's hue/saturation band (see SKILL.md step 3). These are starting systems, not
locked themes — push them toward the specific subject.

Font pairings assume Google Fonts (already how the network loads type). Always pair a head font
with **character** against a clean, readable body. Avoid Inter-on-Inter and the `Sora`+`Inter`
default that every current site shares.

---

## 1. Legal
*Sites:* legalclaimcalc.org, litigationdeadline.com, settlementcalculator.pro, injurycalctools.com, legalformsbystate.com, immigrationcalc.com

- **Feel:** authoritative, precise, sober. Think statute and case file, not startup.
- **Head / body:** `Libre Franklin` or `Fraunces` (serious serif) / `Source Sans 3`.
  Alt: `Spectral` head for a courtroom-gravitas serif.
- **Accent band:** deep, low-saturation — navy, oxblood, forest. HSL S 25–45%, L 25–40%.
  No bright or playful hues.
- **Edges/shadow:** square or 4px radius, hairline rules, no soft shadows. Ruled-paper logic.
- **Signature element:** a precise deadline/result readout — a date or dollar figure set large
  in the serif, framed like a docket stamp.  
- **Hero mascot:** a friendly gavel or balance-scale character, or a wax-sealed verdict stamp — sober, not silly.

## 2. Insurance & Medical
*Sites:* petinsuranceanswers.com, medigapexplained.com, medicareadvantageexplained.com, bizinsurancecost.com, medicalcodesearch.com, cat-insurance.org

- **Feel:** calm, trustworthy, clinical-but-human. Reassurance, not alarm.
- **Head / body:** `Newsreader` or `Bitter` / `Public Sans`. Clinical alt: `Manrope` head.
- **Accent band:** healthcare blues and teals, or a single warm coral for the pet side.
  S 35–55%, L 40–55%. Keep one accent only.
- **Edges/shadow:** soft 10–12px radius, one gentle shadow reserved for the primary card.
- **Signature element:** a clean comparison strip or a single reassuring stat block ("covers
  X of Y") — generous whitespace around it.  
- **Hero mascot:** a rounded shield or umbrella character (insurance), or a cartoon pet for the pet-insurance side.

## 3. Tax & Finance
*Sites:* 1031exchangecalc.com, estatetaxcalculator.pro, tax-calculators.pro, qbicalculator.com, selfemploymentcalculator.com, zipcodesalestax.com, settlementcalculator.pro

- **Feel:** ledger, statement, accuracy. Money is serious and tabular.
- **Head / body:** `Archivo` or `IBM Plex Sans` head / `IBM Plex Sans` or `Inter Tight` body.
  Numbers in a tabular-figures font (`IBM Plex Mono` for the result figure).
- **Accent band:** muted green (money) or slate-blue, desaturated. S 20–40%, L 30–45%.
  Never a gradient.
- **Edges/shadow:** crisp 6px radius, ruled tables with hairline borders, no card shadows.
- **Signature element:** the result as a big monospaced figure with a thin underline rule,
  like a total on a statement.  
- **Hero mascot:** a coin or stack-of-bills character, or a ledger/receipt with a tiny face — shaded, calm palette.

## 4. Home & Construction
*Sites:* concretecalctools.com (the model), homeimprovcalc.com, homeprojectscost.com, costtomoveguide.com, accompressorreplacementcost.net, catalyticconverterreplacementcost.net

- **Feel:** rugged, physical, built. This is concretecalctools' lane — match its conviction.
- **Head / body:** `Oswald` or `Anton` (condensed/industrial) UPPERCASE heads / `Inter` or
  `Roboto` body. Stencil alt: `Saira Condensed`.
- **Accent band:** safety-orange, amber, hi-vis yellow, or steel-blue. S 60–85%, L 45–55% —
  this archetype is allowed to be loud.
- **Edges/shadow:** chunky, slightly squared, strong header rule (concretecalc's `3px orange`
  works *because* it's topic-true here, not as a network default).
- **Signature element:** an oversized uppercase hero with a hazard/measurement motif; the tool
  styled like a worksite readout.  
- **Hero mascot:** a hard-hat tool character — cement mixer, wrench, or measuring tape — chunky and confident.

## 5. Pet & Lifestyle
*Sites:* petinsuranceanswers.com (lifestyle side), pregnancy-tools.com, caloriecalctools.com, exercisecalc.com

- **Feel:** warm, friendly, approachable. Domestic and human.
- **Head / body:** `Quicksand` or `Baloo 2` (rounded) / `Nunito Sans`. Humanist alt: `Epilogue`.
- **Accent band:** warm and bright — coral, terracotta-but-earned, soft teal, marigold.
  S 50–70%, L 55–65%.
- **Edges/shadow:** rounded 14–18px, soft single shadow okay here, generous spacing.
- **Signature element:** one friendly illustrative motif or a warm hero photo; rounded tool card.  
- **Hero mascot:** an actual animal or baby-themed character — this archetype leans hardest into a cute mascot.

## 6. Cosmetic & Health
*Sites:* blepharoplastycost.net, dentalcrowncost.net, dentalimplantcost.co, cremationcost.net (somber register)

- **Feel:** clean, premium, restrained. Editorial, not clinical. **Plain and sourced** for
  cosmetic/end-of-life topics — no hype (see encore-writer register rules).
- **Head / body:** `Cormorant Garamond` or `Fraunces` (elegant serif) / `Inter` light.
- **Accent band:** muted rose, sage, warm taupe, soft gold. S 15–35%, L 50–65%. Very restrained.
  For cremation/end-of-life: near-neutral, one quiet accent, no brightness.
- **Edges/shadow:** generous whitespace, hairline rules, minimal or no shadow, lots of air.
- **Signature element:** an elegant cost-range readout in the serif; editorial spacing.  
- **Hero mascot:** usually NONE — keep it tasteful. An elegant line-icon or a single refined object beats a cartoon here, especially for end-of-life topics.

## 7. Logistics & Industrial
*Sites:* classyourfreight.com, ship-compare.com, printdpicalculator.com, utilcalc.com, businesscalc.pro

- **Feel:** technical, precise, systems-y. Weigh station / spec sheet.
- **Head / body:** `Saira` or `Chivo` / `Inter` or `IBM Plex Sans`. Mono accents for codes.
- **Accent band:** safety-orange, signal-yellow, or industrial blue. S 55–80%, L 45–55%.
- **Edges/shadow:** square edges, monospace data, grid lines, no soft shadows.
- **Signature element:** a spec-sheet-style result block with labeled fields and mono values.  
- **Hero mascot:** a box truck, forklift, or stamped freight crate (see classyourfreight example) — shaded steel + signal accent.

## 8. Generic-Utility fallback
*Use only when a site fits none of the above.*

- **Feel:** clean and competent, but still pick a point of view from the domain name.
- **Head / body:** rotate the head font by domain hash across `{Archivo, Space Grotesk,
  Fraunces, Bricolage Grotesque, Sora-only-if-nothing-else-uses-it}` so siblings differ.
- **Accent band:** hash the hue freely, S 40–60%, L 45–55%, but **never** land on the banned
  purple/violet gradient defaults.
- **Edges/shadow:** one consistent restrained choice; hairline rules over shadows.
- **Signature element:** make the primary tool itself the hero — oversized, one accent.  
- **Hero mascot:** optional — derive an object from the domain noun (e.g. a stopwatch for a timer tool); skip it if nothing fits cleanly.

---

## Banned across all archetypes

- Purple/violet hero gradients (`#8b5cf6`, `#7c3aed`, indigo→pink). The current network leans
  on `#8b5cf6` (gpacalctools) — retire it unless the topic truly is purple.
- Cream + terracotta as a default combo (fine only where *earned*, e.g. pet warmth).
- Emojis in headings.
- Three identical cards as the default section.
- A drop shadow on every box.
- The verbatim `--ink/--accent/--accent2/--accent-dk` token block shared across sites — rename
  and vary it so the template footprint differs domain to domain.

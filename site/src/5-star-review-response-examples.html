5 star review response examples

The best reply to a five-star review is two sentences: thank the customer, then repeat one specific thing they mentioned so it doesn't read like a copy-paste. Skip the exclamation points and the "we're so blessed" language, it reads as fake even when it isn't.

Ten worked examples of responding to positive reviews below, sorted by trade so you can find something close to your own situation fast. Want the negative-review version too, or need a reply written from scratch? Paste the review and let the generator write it, or start further upstream with the review request templates that get more five-star reviews in the door.

Home services and contractors

REVIEW: "Fixed our AC same day in July heat, absolute lifesavers."Glad we could get to you the same day, especially in that heat. Thanks for trusting us with it.
REVIEW: "New roof looks incredible and the crew cleaned up every nail."That cleanup matters to us as much as the roof does. Really appreciate you noticing, and enjoy the new roof.
REVIEW: "Plumber explained exactly what was wrong before touching anything."That's exactly how we want every visit to go. Thanks for the kind words, and call us anytime something comes up.

Dental and medical

REVIEW: "Dr. Reyes made my kid's first cleaning actually fun."Dr. Reyes is going to love hearing that. Thanks for trusting us with your kid's first visit, we'll see you both again soon.
REVIEW: "Barely felt the shot, best dental visit I've had."That's the goal every time. Thanks for the review, and we'll see you at your next cleaning.

Salon and spa

REVIEW: "Jamie nailed the color on the first try, exactly what I pictured."Jamie's going to be thrilled to read this. Thanks for coming in, and we'll see you at your next appointment.
REVIEW: "Best massage I've had in years, already booked my next one."So glad it hit the spot. Thanks for booking again, we'll see you soon.

Auto repair

REVIEW: "Diagnosed the noise in ten minutes that two other shops missed."That noise had been bugging you a while, glad we finally tracked it down. Thanks for giving us the shot.
REVIEW: "Honest quote, no upsell, done by end of day."That's exactly how we try to run things. Appreciate you noticing, and thanks for the review.

Restaurant and hospitality

REVIEW: "The short rib special was unreal and our server remembered our anniversary."Chef's going to hear about that short rib, and I'll pass along the note about your anniversary too. Thanks for celebrating with us.
REVIEW: "Patio was quiet enough for a real conversation, food came out fast."Glad the patio worked out for you. Thanks for the review, hope to see you back soon.

Privacy policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

When you sign up we collect your name, business name, cell number, and email so we can set up and run your service. We use them for that, and for nothing else. We do not sell or rent your information.

When you send us a customer's name and number, we use it to send the review messages you hired us to send, and we stop using it when the sequence ends or the person opts out. Recipients can opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP, and opt-out lists are honored permanently. We do not market to your customers, and we do not share their contact details with anyone.

The website uses Google Analytics and Vercel Web Analytics to count visits and see which pages get read. That data is about browsers, not names, and we use it to make the site better. No analytics data is sold or shared beyond those two providers, and none of it touches the customer lists you send us.

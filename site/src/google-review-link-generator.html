Quick answers

How do I get my Google review link?

Search your business name above and pick your listing; the tool builds the direct review link from your Google Place ID. Or open your Google Business Profile, tap "Ask for reviews," and paste that link here to make the QR code.

Is this really free?

Yes. The link, the QR code, and the printable card are free with no signup. We make our money on a paid service that sends review requests automatically, and we're happy to earn your attention this way first.

Where should I put the QR code?

Wherever a happy customer stands still for a moment: the front counter, the invoice, the receipt, the back of a business card, the job-site sign, or the thank-you email. One scan takes them straight to the five-star form.

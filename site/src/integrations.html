The do-it-yourself version (5 minutes)

Your personal webhook address arrives when you join (or text us and we'll resend it). Then in Zapier:

STEP 1: TRIGGERPick your app and its "done" moment: Jobber "Job Closed", QuickBooks "Invoice Paid", Square "Payment Completed", or whatever fits how you work.
STEP 2: ACTIONAdd "Webhooks by Zapier" as the action, choose POST, and paste your WellEarnedReviews webhook address as the URL.
STEP 3: FIELDSMap three fields from your app: first (the customer's first name), phone or email (either works, both is better), and job (optional, makes the check-in message specific).

Payload example:
{"first": "Sarah", "phone": "9165550134", "email": "sarah@example.com", "job": "roof replacement"}
THAT'S ITTurn the Zap on. Every completed job now starts the polite four-message sequence: check-in, ask, two spaced reminders. Duplicates are ignored automatically, opt-outs are honored forever, and everyone gets the same review link. Always.

Quick answers

How should I respond to a negative Google review?

Stay calm, thank them for the specifics, own what was actually your fault, say what changes, and take the rest offline with a direct contact. Never argue, never offer compensation in public, and never mention details a customer didn't raise themselves. The generator follows those rules automatically.

Should I respond to positive reviews too?

Yes. A short, specific thank-you signals to Google and to future customers that the business is alive and paying attention. Two sentences is plenty; mention the detail they praised so it doesn't read like a form letter.

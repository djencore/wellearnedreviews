# WER Competitor Keyword Gap Analysis

**Date:** 2026-07-12. Method: three parallel research agents pulled per-domain organic data for 8 competitors. Sources: Ahrefs (nicejob.com, gatherup.com, starloop.com, podium.com, birdeye.com), Semrush US (reviewgrower.com, usehatchapp.com, signpost.com; its API approval cleared mid-run). Shortlist re-verified in one Ahrefs keywords-explorer batch, US. CPCs in USD. WER baseline at pull time: 0 organic keywords (domain is days old), so every candidate below is a true gap.

| Domain | Authority | US organic traffic/mo | Keywords | What actually ranks for them |

| birdeye.com | DR 85 | 364,408 | 105,169 | Blog articles (review examples, verticals) plus off-topic name-generator tools; product pages barely rank |

The July 11 census conclusion holds with fuller data: the incumbents win review-adjacent SEO with content archetypes (troubleshooting posts, template libraries, comparison pages), not authority, and several rank weakly on their highest-value terms.

## Prioritized page queue (verified numbers: Ahrefs vol/KD/CPC, 2026-07-12)

### Tier 1: bottom-funnel money pages (build first)

1. **/birdeye-pricing** target "birdeye pricing" 7,227 vol / KD 6 / $18 CPC. The single best ROI target in the dataset. Honest breakdown of their per-location pricing and annual-contract terms, then the WER comparison. Adjacent: "birdeye reviews" 11,000 / KD 39 for later.
2. **/birdeye-alternative** 150 vol / KD 0 / $13-25 CPC. NiceJob owns this today with a thin /compare/ page. Validated Phase D target.
3. **/podium-pricing** 421 / KD 6 / $5 and **/podium-alternative** 40-150 / KD 0 / $13-15. "podium competitors" 100 / KD 0 / $17 rides along. One page can hold alternative+competitors variants.
4. **"podium vs birdeye"** 100+100 (both directions) / KD 0 / $10. A neutral head-to-head that positions WER as the third option.

### Tier 2: troubleshooting and how-to posts (exact ICP, KD 0-10)

5. **Google reviews not showing up** cluster: "why is my google review not showing" 355 / KD 0 plus "google reviews not showing up" 161 / KD 0, traffic potential 2,100. NiceJob's single post here ranks for 148 keywords; it is their best non-brand page and beatable.
6. **How to send a Google review link** 172 / KD 10, traffic potential 12,000. Internal-links straight to the live link generator tool. GatherUp ranks 1 today with a blog post.
7. **Leave a Google review for a business** 694 / KD 1. End-user explainer (the Wiremo pattern): builds topical authority even though searchers are not buyers.

### Tier 3: extend clusters that already exist on the site

8. **Response-examples cluster extension:** "satisfied customer reviews examples" 1,505 / KD 11 and "positive review examples" variants. Podium absorbs these with one hub; WER already has 5 posts and needs 2-3 more targeting these exact heads.
9. **Templates page coverage check:** "ask for google review template" 150 / KD 1, "google review text message" 144 / KD 0, "review request template" 119 / KD 3. The live /review-request-templates page should explicitly carry these phrasings (title/H2/FAQ), likely a copy tweak rather than a new page.
10. **Reputation marketing guide** 1,186 / KD 6 / $10 CPC, traffic potential 44,000. NiceJob ranks 1 with a blog guide. Category education that feeds the funnel.

### Tier 4: product-category and vertical pages

11. **Small dedicated pages:** "google review software" 246 / KD 5 / $9, "review generation" 223 / KD 0 / $10, "best reputation management software" 268 / KD 4 / $13 (traffic potential 7,400).
12. **Vertical review-management pages** (the Birdeye pattern, KD 2-3): "hotel review management" 255 / KD 3 / $11, "dental review management" KD 2 / $10. Pairs with the CRM's industry field and the industry benchmark line planned for Phase 6.
13. **Free reputation report** 263 / KD 2 / $3.50. Lands exactly on the free review-audit lead magnet already on the build queue from the GatherUp teardown (their Review Defense).

### Tier 5: trade template white space (the Hatch move, validated)

14. Hatch ranks 1-8 on trade contract templates (KD 9-35, $4-15 CPC) with two blog posts and has zero review keywords. Nobody does **review-request templates per trade** (HVAC, roofing, dental, auto). This is Phase E with fresh evidence that the mechanic works on WER's exact ICP.

## Watchouts

- **Buy-reviews adjacency:** reviewgrower, signpost, and even gatherup rank on "buy google/yelp/facebook reviews" terms (up to 4,400 vol). Never target these. The brand is the compliance story.
- **Agency/white-label terms** (GatherUp's $9-15 CPC cluster, KD 3-12) are real but a different ICP (agencies resell). Park until WER wants an agency tier.
- NiceJob topline traffic is inflated by customer microsites; do not overweight their authority.
- Semrush and Ahrefs KD disagree (Semrush reads 20-40 points higher on some terms); numbers above are Ahrefs-verified where both existed.
- Data is directional. Treat page-level traffic estimates as ratios.

## How this merges with the existing roadmap

Phases A (tools) and B (response examples) are live. This analysis reorders what comes next: Tier 1 pricing/comparison pages are cheaper and higher-intent than the Phase C cornerstone guide, so build them first (four pages, KD 0-6, $10-25 CPC), then the Tier 2 troubleshooting posts, then resume Phase C ("how to get more google reviews" 600 / KD 24-31) once a few referring domains exist. Tier 5 replaces guesswork in Phase E with Hatch's proven numbers.
